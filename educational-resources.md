# Educational Resources

Free learning platforms, courses, and research tools for students.

## Online Learning Platforms

| Platform | Description | Access Link |
|----------|-------------|-------------|
| **Harvard Online Learning** | Free courses from Harvard University | [Harvard PLL](https://pll.harvard.edu/catalog/free) |
| **University of Moratuwa** | Open educational resources from UoM | [Open UoM](https://open.uom.lk/) |
| **edX** | Massive open online courses from top universities | [edX Free Courses](https://www.edx.org/school/edx) |

## Research & Development Tools

| Tool/Service | Description | Access Link |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|
| **NotebookLM** | AI-powered research and note-taking assistant | [NotebookLM](https://notebooklm.google.com/) |
| **Devpost** | Platform for discovering and participating in international online hackathons | [Devpost](https://devpost.com/) |

## Learning Tips

- **Harvard & edX**: Offer verified certificates (often paid) but course content is free to audit
- **NotebookLM**: Upload research papers and get AI-powered summaries and insights
- **Devpost**: Great way to build your portfolio and network with developers worldwide
